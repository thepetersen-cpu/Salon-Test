Stripe Plan

Customers pay on a secure Stripe page — cards, Apple Pay & Google Pay accepted. Stripe emails them a receipt automatically. After they pay, verify in your Stripe Dashboard, then click Mark Received.

How it works

  1. Enter total & pick installments → we calculate the per-payment amount
  2. Create a Stripe Payment Link for that amount (takes 1 minute)
  3. Paste it here → we store it in Firebase
  4. Click Send to Customer each time a payment is due
  5. Confirm payment in Stripe, then click Mark Received

Each installment

Go to Stripe → Payment Links → Create link for . Copy the URL and paste it here.
